# Let's Apply Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Let's Apply stores information that users choose to provide, including account details, career profiles, CV content, saved jobs, application records, and a compressed profile image.

This information is used to provide vacancy discovery, profile management, job matching, document generation, saved jobs, and application tracking. Let's Apply does not silently submit applications or send email without user approval.

CV and cover-letter PDFs are generated locally on the user's device. Information shared with an employer, email provider, or external career website is also governed by that recipient's privacy practices.

Users can edit their information or permanently delete their account and associated profile, saved jobs, and application records from the Profile screen.

Vacancy matches and generated application content are decision-support tools. Users must review all generated content for accuracy before submitting it.

This policy must be published at a public HTTPS address before App Store submission and that address must be entered in App Store Connect.
