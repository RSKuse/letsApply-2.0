Let's Apply Privacy Policy

Effective 30 June 2026

Let's Apply helps users discover vacancies, prepare career documents, and track applications. This policy explains how career data is handled.

Information you provide

The service stores information you choose to provide, including your account details, career profile, CV content, saved jobs, application records, and a small compressed profile image.

How information is used

Your information is used to provide vacancy discovery, profile management, job matching, document generation, saved jobs, and application tracking. Let's Apply does not silently submit applications or send email without your approval.

Generated documents and external services

CV and cover-letter PDFs are generated locally on your device. When you share a document or continue to an employer website or email provider, that recipient's privacy practices also apply.

Vacancy sources

Vacancies may be curated by Let's Apply, supplied by recruiters, or imported from identified public sources. Source links and application instructions are displayed where available. Users should verify employer instructions before submission.

Data control and deletion

You can edit your information at any time. The Profile screen includes a Delete Account command that removes your Firebase sign-in account, career profile, saved jobs, and application records.

Security

Access to user profiles, saved jobs, and applications is restricted through Firebase Authentication and Firestore security rules. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Decision-support notice

Job matches and generated application content are decision-support tools, not guarantees of employment. Review every statement and document for accuracy before use.

Questions

Privacy and support questions can be submitted through the Let's Apply support page.
